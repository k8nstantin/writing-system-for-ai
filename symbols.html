The Law of Semantic Normalization: Every written form corresponds to exactly one distinct core meaning. This catalog lists all active operational symbols and proposed expansions. In Alan, rather than multiplying unique symbols endlessly, we use Spatial Modifiers (D-Pad compass stars) and Logical Modifiers (ANTI crowns) to morph core concepts elegantly into new words.
🧭 The 8-Position Compass Modifier (D-Pad Expansion)
In our latest iteration, the D-Pad acts as a **Suffix Modifier** on any preceding symbol! Instead of drawing standalone directional arrows, tapping a D-Pad key attaches the **4-pointed Compass Star (`✦`)** at specific coordinates surrounding the base symbol. This supports up to **8 discrete spatial directions**:
  • Above: Floating at Top-Center
  • Below: Floating at Bottom-Center
  • Left / Right: Floating at Left/Right Center
  • Diagonal Corners (8 Positions): E.g. Top-Right, Bottom-Left
  • Inside (Frame Brackets): Instead of an interior star (which clashes with fills), "Inside" encloses the symbol inside four elegant corner L-brackets!
