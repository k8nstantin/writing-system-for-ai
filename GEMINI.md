## Foundational Alan Semiotic Rules

### 1. The Suffix-Append Modifier Paradigm (D-Pad Compass Keys)
- **Direct Spatial Modification:** Tapping D-pad keys (Above, Below, Left, Right, Inside) does not type a standalone symbol. Instead, it acts as a suffix modifier on the preceding symbol inside Box 2.
- **4-Pointed Compass Star (✦):** Attaches floating at top center (`above`), bottom center (`below`), left center (`left`), or right center (`right`) of the target symbol.
- **Inside Framing Brackets ([ ]):** Tapping `Inside` (in) encloses the symbol inside four elegant corner L-brackets to completely eliminate overlapping/clashing with filled icons!
- **Combinatorial 8-Positions:** Support compound diagonals by attaching multiple stars to the margins.

### 2. Symmetrical Floating "Air-Gap" Diacritics
- **3-Unit Vertical Gap:** The bottom of Zone 2 negation crowns (`★`) must end exactly at `y = -1` (above top-most peak of symbols at `y = 2`). Bottom crowns must sit symmetrically at `y = 25` (floating below symbol bases at `y = 22`). This prevents any visual merger and preserves immaculate, clean geometric separation.
- **4-Unit Horizontal Gap:** Left-margin stars must maintain a clear 4-unit gap (`x = -4`) to avoid clashing with vertical glyph segments.

### 3. Spatial Indentation & Down Hierarchy (AST Mapping)
- **Root Level (Level 0):** Used exclusively for core substantives (subjects / nouns / times contexts).
- **Indent (Level +1):** Indicates logical dependency, modification, or action scoping (similar to programming code blocks).
- **New Line (DOWN):** Represents sequential progression, subsequent steps, or sibling clauses.
- **Syntactic Noise Elimination:** Traditional grammatical helper words (brackets, conjunctions, case markers) are completely eliminated; the sentence's grammatical tree (AST) is telegraphed purely by physical 2D indentation on the page!
